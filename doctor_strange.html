Doctor Strange is a brilliant but arrogant surgeon who, after a car accident that damages his hands, discovers the world of magic and becomes a powerful sorcerer. He trains under the Ancient One, a powerful mystic, and becomes the Sorcerer Supreme, tasked with protecting Earth from mystical threats.Doctor Strange is known for his mastery of the mystic arts, including astral projection, time manipulation, and interdimensional travel. He is often depicted wearing a red cloak and wielding the Eye of Agamotto, a powerful artifact that can manipulate time and reveal truth.
