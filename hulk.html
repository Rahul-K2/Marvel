The Hulk is a superhero who possesses incredible physical strength and an ability to regenerate his body quickly. The character is the alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner, a brilliant scientist who was exposed to gamma radiation, which triggered a transformation that turns him into the Hulk whenever he becomes angry or agitated. The Hulk is often depicted as a giant, green-skinned creature with immense strength, durability, and an almost unlimited level of stamina. Despite his intimidating appearance and temper, the Hulk has a strong moral compass and is often seen fighting to protect the innocent and defend humanity from various threats.
